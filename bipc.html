What is BiPC?

BiPC stands for Biology, Physics and Chemistry. It is suitable for students interested in healthcare, medical science and research.

Subjects

  • Biology
  • Physics
  • Chemistry

Skills Required

  • Observation Skills
  • Patience
  • Scientific Thinking

Courses

  • MBBS
  • BDS
  • B.Pharmacy
  • B.Sc Nursing
  • Biotechnology

Career Options

  • Doctor
  • Dentist
  • Pharmacist
  • Biotechnologist
  • Nutritionist
  • Physiotherapist

Future Scope

Healthcare, Biotechnology, Medical Research and Allied Health Sciences.
