What is CEC?

CEC stands for Civics, Economics and Commerce. It is suitable for students interested in business, law, finance and government careers.

  • Economics
  • Commerce

Skills Required

  • Leadership
  • Decision Making

  • BBA
  • CA
  • LLB

Career Options

  • Lawyer
  • Bank Officer
  • Business Manager
  • IAS Officer
  • Entrepreneur

Future Scope

Business, Finance, Law, Government Services and Entrepreneurship.
